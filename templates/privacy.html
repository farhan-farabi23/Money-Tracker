Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or any financial account credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Money Tracker service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and personalising your experience. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical precautions to protect it from unauthorised access, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Money Tracker does not share your personal data with third parties. We use Google Fonts to load typefaces — this results in a request to Google's servers when you visit the site, subject to Google's own privacy policy. No expense data is transmitted to any third party.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to request deletion of your data, please reach out through the app's support channel. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
